# Launch Strategy

## Launch Tiers

| Tier | When | Effort | Channels |
|------|------|--------|----------|
| **Tier 1: Big Launch** | New product, major pivot | 4-6 weeks prep | All channels, PR, Product Hunt |
| **Tier 2: Feature Launch** | Major feature | 1-2 weeks prep | Email, blog, social, changelog |
| **Tier 3: Minor Update** | Small improvements | Same day | Changelog, in-app notification |

## Tier 1: Full Launch Checklist

### 4 Weeks Before
- [ ] Landing page with email capture
- [ ] Launch blog post draft
- [ ] Demo video / screenshots
- [ ] Social media teaser content (3-5 posts)
- [ ] Email sequence for waitlist (3 emails)

### 1 Week Before
- [ ] Product Hunt listing prepared (if applicable)
- [ ] Email to existing users / waitlist
- [ ] Social posts scheduled
- [ ] Reach out to 10 people for Day 1 support

### Launch Day
- [ ] Publish landing page / open signups
- [ ] Send launch email
- [ ] Post on Product Hunt (Tuesday-Thursday, 12:01am PT)
- [ ] Post on social channels
- [ ] Post in relevant communities (HN, Reddit, Discord, Indie Hackers)
- [ ] Monitor and respond to every comment

### Post-Launch (7 days)
- [ ] Share Day 1 results publicly (builds momentum)
- [ ] Follow up with everyone who signed up
- [ ] Write "behind the scenes" content
- [ ] Collect and publish testimonials
- [ ] Analyze conversion funnel, fix drop-offs

## Product Hunt Optimization
- Build relationships with hunters 2+ weeks before
- Prepare: tagline (60 chars), description, 5 images, maker comment
- Launch Tuesday-Thursday for best visibility
- First 4 hours are critical — rally your network
- Reply to every comment within 30 minutes

## ORB Channel Strategy
| Type | Channel | Cost |
|------|---------|------|
| **Owned** | Email list, blog, app, social | Free |
| **Rented** | Paid ads, sponsored content | Paid |
| **Borrowed** | PR, guest posts, communities, influencers | Time |

Start with Owned. Only spend on Rented after Owned channels work.
