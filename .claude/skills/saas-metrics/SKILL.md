# SaaS Metrics

## The Metrics That Matter

### Revenue
| Metric | Formula | Healthy |
|--------|---------|---------|
| **MRR** | Sum of monthly recurring revenue | Growing month-over-month |
| **ARR** | MRR × 12 | — |
| **MRR Growth** | (MRR now - MRR last) / MRR last | >10% early, >5% growth stage |
| **Net Revenue Retention** | (Start MRR + Expansion - Churn) / Start MRR | >100% (means growth without new customers) |

### Customers
| Metric | Formula | Healthy |
|--------|---------|---------|
| **Churn Rate** | Customers lost / Total customers | <5% monthly, <2% ideal |
| **Logo Churn** | Accounts cancelled / Total accounts | Lower is better |
| **Revenue Churn** | Revenue lost / Total MRR | Can be negative (good!) with expansion |

### Unit Economics
| Metric | Formula | Healthy |
|--------|---------|---------|
| **CAC** | Sales + Marketing spend / New customers | — |
| **LTV** | ARPU / Monthly churn rate | — |
| **LTV:CAC** | LTV / CAC | >3:1 |
| **CAC Payback** | CAC / Monthly ARPU | <12 months |

### Growth Efficiency
| Metric | Formula | Healthy |
|--------|---------|---------|
| **Burn Multiple** | Net burn / Net new ARR | <2x good, <1x great |
| **Magic Number** | Net new ARR / S&M spend (prev quarter) | >0.75 |
| **Rule of 40** | Revenue growth % + Profit margin % | >40% |

## Quick Health Check
```
Revenue growing >10% MoM?        → ✅ or 🔴
Churn <5% monthly?               → ✅ or 🔴
LTV:CAC >3:1?                    → ✅ or 🔴
CAC Payback <12 months?          → ✅ or 🔴
Net Revenue Retention >100%?     → ✅ or 🔴
```

3+ red = urgent fix needed. Focus on the worst metric first.

## Fundraising Readiness by Stage
| Stage | ARR | Growth | Churn | LTV:CAC |
|-------|-----|--------|-------|---------|
| Pre-seed | $0-50K | — | — | — |
| Seed | $50K-500K | >20% MoM | <10% | >2:1 |
| Series A | $1M-3M | >15% MoM | <5% | >3:1 |
| Series B | $5M-15M | >100% YoY | <3% | >3:1 |
